Frequently Asked Questions
How do I subscribe to the tiffin service?
Contact us via WhatsApp at +91 99818 11407 or call directly. We'll set up your plan and start delivery from the next day.
What are the delivery timings?
Lunch: 12:00 PM – 1:30 PM, Dinner: 7:00 PM – 8:30 PM (Monday to Sunday).
Do you provide dinner as well?
Yes! Our Premium Plan includes 2 meals per day — both lunch and dinner.
How can I place an order?
Click "Order on WhatsApp" or call +91 99818 11407. We'll confirm delivery details immediately.

Yes, both veg and non-veg tiffins available. Standard & Premium plans include non-veg options.
